Caipirinha

Wanna get drunk in style? Show your birdie That you can make a few drinks? Follow me

Ingredients

  • A bottle of cachaça
  • A whole lemon
  • Sugar

How to make it

  1. In a cup, put the slices of lemon in it
  2. Now, put 3 tablespoons of sugar in the cup
  3. Gently mash the lemon in it, to release its juice
  4. Now add 100ml of Cachaça and mix it
  5. Drink and enjoy ya freak
