Chicken Strogonoff

Chicken Strogonoff is a very popular brazilian dish, originated in Russia, but adapted with the south american flavor which gives it such a good vibe to it!

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • One Onion
  • 500g of Chicken Breast
  • Worcester Sauce
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Heavy Cream

How to make it

  1. Chop the onions into little cubes
  2. Wash the rice
  3. In another pan, use half the onions until its brown
  4. Take the onions out and cook the chicken
  5. While the chicken is cooking, also cook the rice!
  6. Once the chicken is cooked, put 250ml of Tomato Sauce in the pan
  7. Now add Worcester Sauce and Heavy Cream in the pan
  8. Cook on low temperature for 10 more minutes
  9. Your chicken Strogonoff is ready, enjoy!
